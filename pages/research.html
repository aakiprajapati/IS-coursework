Gift Section

The reference for the gift section was taken from the Piaget 150 website. While they included gift sections for men, women, and by colours, our website has its sections for men, women and for both. Our website also consists of background for images while the reference website does not. We have also included watch names, that could be a good idea to be gifted.

Motto Section

The reference for the gift section was taken from the Piaget 150 website. While they included a small message for the users for the upcoming festivals, our website consists of the relation between the business name and watches as well as our motto.

Nav Bar

The reference for the above nav bar section was taken from the titan watch nepal's website. While they had a scrolling attribute which led to a different banner our website only dispalys one banner and doesn't have any animation attribute. We included the above nav section because it showcases the advantage a customer could get if he/she purchases the product.


Structure of Form

The reference for the survey form was taken from the webpage called cloudinary. The reference taken was just the basic structure of the form and not the overall structure as you can see from the image depicted above. The survey form we created asks the user related to their experience of exploration of our website. While the questions might also be similar it is to be noted that they are not copied.

Product Section

The design of the Product page was inspired by the Melbourne Watch Company Website. Melbourne Watch Company is known for its elegant and minimalist design, emphasizing luxury and sophistication, which perfectly fits our vintage and luxury watch collection. Using this reference, we created our own design using different CSS properties and modified the product card design too. Using our creativity, we took the concepts and techniques from this website and applied them to our own web page.
